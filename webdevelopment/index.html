"Small Towns, Big Stories"

Welcome to the Missouri Photo Workshop’s legacy exhibit.

This online exhibit features one photograph from the first 75 workshops that have been held in more than 50 small towns across Missouri.

Each year, 40 photographers gather to find a story worth sharing. Under the guidance of professional photo editors, the photojournalists tell important stories in each town and capture the spirt of each community. This exhibit can be viewed online, and you can also bring it to your town. Contact us if you are interested in having the framed prints displayed in your community.
